Melbourne City Analytics on Art


Among the 200 most frequent word tokens from over 2 million tweets collected.

We decided to study the correlation of people’s attitude on art and other aspects of their lives. Art, by its definition, is a wide range of human activities in creating visual, auditory or performing artefacts, expressing the author’s imaginative and conceptual ideas.

It is a beautiful yet controversial concept. We are curious about whether people’s attitude on arts can potentially be correlated with their income level, how much they value tertiary education and their willingness to make donations.
