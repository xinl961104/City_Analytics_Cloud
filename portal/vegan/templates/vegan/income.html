From our experience, we always have the stereotype on the people who show interest in art. We might assume that art is for rich people, but there is no grounding evidence for that. This topic is established to find out whether higher income level have any correlation with people’s attitude on art in an area. We compared the average income of people in dollars per year of every region. From the result obtained, we observe that, although with some outliers, but generally the higher the average income, the more positive people’s attitude on art can be. Art is really for rich people!
