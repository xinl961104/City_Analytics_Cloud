Donation Overview:

From our experience, we consider people who appreciate art to be more emotional, which means they are more sensitive in terms of having empathy for others in pain. To reach out a hand to a stranger, the best way to do in this modern society is to donate.
In this topic, we want to find out whether people’s attitude on art has any correlation with their willingness to donate, or in other words, are people who appreciate art of more empathy? We compared the median amount of donation in dollar people make in every region.
The results show that, although with some outlier, but generally, the more positive people’s attitude on art is, the more donation people are willing to make. Therefore, art might really make people have more empathy.
